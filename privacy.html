Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Max Effort Apps ("we", "our", "us") operates mobile applications including Slide Rush. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Our apps may collect the following types of information:

  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage data such as session duration, game scores, and feature usage to help us improve the app experience. This is collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Device identifiers: Advertising identifiers used by our ad partners to serve relevant ads.
  • Leaderboard data: If you participate in leaderboards or multiplayer, we store game scores and anonymized player identifiers via Firebase.

Advertising

Our free apps display ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your preferences and device settings. You can learn more about Google's data practices at Google Privacy Policy.

You may purchase a "Remove Ads" option within the app to disable all advertising.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by the respective platform.

Data Storage

Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. Leaderboard and multiplayer data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore with industry-standard security rules.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Analytics, Auth, Firestore)
  • Google AdMob

Your Choices

  • You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • You can purchase "Remove Ads" to stop all advertising in the app.
  • You can delete your local data by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at maxeffortapps@gmail.com.
